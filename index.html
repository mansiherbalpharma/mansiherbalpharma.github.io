Our Story

Before you start going distance with us, it is important to get familiar. Therefore, hold tight because the moment we all were waiting is here, by this post we officially start our step towards digitalization.
The journey of the company (Mansi Herbal Pharma) started back in 2008, by visionary & founder Mr. Shyam S. Jhalani who had the aim of bringing the traditional science of Ayurveda at par with the contemporary world. The 5000-year-old science that originated in the Vedic age began to lose its charisma in the times that followed. However, with the revolutionized changes that happened in the acquisition and retention of this knowledge crowned Ayurveda with its original glory. We think of ourselves as a small initiative, which intends to add some more stars to this glory. We provide you with an opportunity to make a choice between the artificially created products and the naturally prepared products, by bringing a range of over 80+ products right at your doorstep. With our expanding range of products and growth in the market, we are committed to enrich the lives of people who use out products.


Establish Mansi Herbal Pharma as a science-based, problem-solving, head-to-heel brand, harnessed from nature's wealth and characterized by trust and healthy lives.
Develop markets worldwide with an in-depth and long-term approach, maintaining at each step the highest ethical standards.
Respect, collaborate with and utilize the talents of each member of the Mansi Herbal Pharma family and the local communities where Mansi Herbal Pharma products are developed and/or consumed, to drive our seed-to-shelf policy and to rigorously adopt eco-friendly practices to support the environment we inhabit.
Ensure that each Mansi Herbal Pharma strongly backs our promise to exceed the expectations of the customer, each and every time. Nothing less is acceptable.
Caring for life is Mansi Herbal Pharma's core business philosophy. It defines the way we think and the way we act.
We, as a company, wish to improve the lives of people and leave a positive impact on the communities that make Mansi Herbal Pharma the brand it is today.
Beyond our products, we wish to touch the lives of people and aim at bringing the change of a better tomorrow through initiatives that empower people and protect the true glory of Mother Earth.
Our founder, Mr. S. S. Jhalani, firmly believed that the success of an organization depends on the people working for the company. The best minds inspire the best innovations. He felt that a company needs bright, passionate individuals who are ready to take on challenges and think “out of the box”. Over the years, this very philosophy has formed the core of our Human Resource practices.
People are Mansi Herbal Pharma's most important asset and investing in them is vital to us. From giving them the creative freedom to push the boundaries of research to fostering a culture of open communication and diversity, we believe that our nurturing work environment brings out the best in our team and allows us to develop the best products.
